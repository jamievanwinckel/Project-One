Mystical Forest Adventure

You are about to start your journey through the mystical forest, but first you must decide what you would like to carry with you just in case you need it. You can buy food and water, which you can carry very easily and always be supplied with your necessary survival needs. Or you can carry money, and you can choose as you go on the journey where you want to spend it. (It is easier to carry the money than the food and water, but bringing the food is the safest bet)
