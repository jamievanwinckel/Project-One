Mystical Forest Adventure

You chose to bring food and you have begun your journey. There is a river that you have to cross, so you start walking across it while holding the food up over your head. Unfortunately, the river is to deep and you cannot stand… So part of your food gets wet. Since your food is now soggy, you have two options. The first option is to find someone to trade with, this is a very good idea if you can find someone who will trade with you. The second option is to eat the food even though it is soggy, this is an okay decision if you don’t mind eating soggy food.
