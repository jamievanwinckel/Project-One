Mystical Forest Adventure

You chose to trade your food, so when you pass by a marketplace you stop and go in to see where you can trade it. You happen to find a leprechaun there who desperately needs food. He doesn’t have money but he has two things he is offering to trade with you: magical crystals or some of his leprechaun eyelashes. Both of these have special powers: the magic crystals make you hyper (you won’t need to rest while traveling), and the leprechaun eyelashes let you see the future (you can change what actually happens after you see it).
