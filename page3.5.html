Mystical Forest Adventure

Now that you bought the magical crystals that make you hyper, you get to decide if you eat the crystals or not. If you do eat them, then you will help the people that are carrying a lot of groceries to their cabin in the woods. And if you help the people, they will give you a hover machine that will guide you straight out of the mystical forest. But you can also save the crystals for another time when they might be more helpful. So, which one will it be?
